### Design Specs:
- **Background:** Black (#000000)
- **Primary Text:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Accent:** Access Red (#e30613)
- **Font:** Barlow Condensed (bold for headers)
- **QR Code Size:** 200x200px minimum
- **Margins:** 20px all sides

### Text Hierarchy:
1. **"CORPORATE CUP 2026"** - 48pt, Bold, White
2. **"PREDICTIONS"** - 36pt, Bold, Red
3. **"WIN A PREMIER LEAGUE SHIRT"** - 32pt, Bold, White
4. **QR Code** - Center, Large
5. **"SCAN TO ENTER"** - 24pt, Bold, Red
6. **URL** - 18pt, Regular, White
7. **Details** - 14pt, Regular, Gray

## Engagement Tips

### Week 1: Launch
- Send initial email
- Display posters
- Mention in team meetings
- Share on social media

### Week 2: Build Momentum
- Share entry count updates
- Highlight donation total
- Create friendly competition between teams
- Share fun facts about players

### Week 3: Final Push
- Send reminder emails
- Update posters with countdown
- Share leaderboard (if appropriate)
- Create urgency

### Tournament Day
- Live updates
- Photos/videos
- Real-time scoring
- Build excitement for winner announcement

### Post-Tournament
- Announce winner
- Share final stats
- Thank participants
- Celebrate donation total
- Plan for next year
