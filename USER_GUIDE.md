# 📱 Corporate Cup Predictor - User Guide

Quick guide for staff making predictions.

---

## 🎯 How to Enter

### Step 1: Open the App
- **Scan QR code** with your phone camera
- Or visit the URL directly
- Works on any device (phone, tablet, computer)

### Step 2: Enter Your Details
- **Full Name**: Your first and last name
- **Email**: Your work email (one entry per email)

### Step 3: Predict Total Goals
- Use **+ / -** buttons or type directly
- Predict **0-99 goals** across all games
- Think about how many games each team plays!

### Step 4: Pick Top Scorers
- Select **one player** from Men's Team
- Select **one player** from Mixed Team
- These are your predicted top goal scorers

### Step 5: Upload Donation Proof
1. Click **"Open Donation Page"**
2. Donate **$10 AUD minimum** to Heartbeat of Football
3. Take a **screenshot** of confirmation
4. Upload the screenshot
5. Click **"Submit Entry"**

### Step 6: Done! ✓
- You'll see a success screen
- Your predictions are recorded
- Good luck! 🏆

---

## 🏆 How to Win

### Prize
**Premier League shirt** of your choice! 🎽

### Winning Criteria
1. **Closest total goals prediction**
2. If tied: **Combined goals by your predicted top scorers** (highest wins)
3. If still tied: **Earliest submission time**

### Example
```
Actual Results:
- Total Goals: 28
- Men's Top Scorer: James Anderson (5 goals)
- Mixed Top Scorer: Sarah Johnson (4 goals)

Your Prediction:
- Total Goals: 27 (1 goal off!)
- Men's Top Scorer: James Anderson ✓
- Mixed Top Scorer: Sarah Johnson ✓
- Combined Scorer Goals: 9

You'd be a strong contender! 🎯
```

## ❓ FAQ

### Can I submit multiple entries?
**No.** One entry per email address. Choose wisely!

### What if I make a mistake?
**Contact admin** before the event. Entries can be deleted and resubmitted.

### When is the deadline?
**Before March 20, 2026** (event day). Submit early!

### What if I don't know the players?
**That's okay!** It's all for fun and charity. Pick your favorites!

### How much should I donate?
**Minimum $10 AUD.** Feel free to donate more! 💝

### Where does my donation go?
**Heartbeat of Football** - a charity supporting football communities.

### When will the winner be announced?
**After the Corporate Cup** on March 20, 2026. Results will be calculated and announced via email.

### Can I change my prediction?
**No.** Once submitted, predictions are final. Think carefully!

### What if two people have the same prediction?
**Tie-breaker rules apply:**
1. Combined goals by predicted scorers
2. Earliest submission time

### Do I need to attend the event?
**No.** You can make predictions and donate without attending. Winner will be contacted via email.

## 💡 Tips for Winning

### Research
- Look at **previous Corporate Cup results**
- Check **team rosters** and player strengths
- Consider **number of games** each team plays

### Strategy
- **Conservative**: Predict 15-25 goals (safer)
- **Aggressive**: Predict 30-40 goals (higher risk)
- **Balanced**: Predict 25-30 goals (middle ground)

### Top Scorers
- Pick **experienced players**
- Consider **strikers and forwards**
- Look at **past performance**

### Timing
- **Submit early** for tie-breaker advantage
- But **research first** for better prediction
- Balance speed vs. accuracy
